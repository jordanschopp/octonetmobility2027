📱 Device Compatibility Check

To check if your phone supports eSIM — go to Settings → General → About and look for an EID number. If it is there, your phone supports eSIM.

💸 Roaming Savings Calculator

See how much you could save vs carrier roaming rates.

📋 eSIM Setup Checklist

✓ Check your phone has eSIM support (look for EID in Settings → About)
✓ Confirm your phone is carrier unlocked (contact your carrier — free in Canada)
✓ Install the OctoNet eSIM at home on Wi-Fi before you travel
✓ Keep your home SIM active for calls and texts if needed
✓ Set OctoNet as your mobile data line in Settings
✓ Turn off Data Roaming on your home SIM to avoid carrier charges
✓ Use Wi-Fi for large downloads and backups
Tools are for guidance only. eSIM compatibility depends on your specific device model and carrier unlock status.
