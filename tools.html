iPhone Setup Guide

  1. Open Settings.
  2. Tap Cellular.
  3. Tap Add eSIM.
  4. Scan QR code or use carrier activation.
  5. Turn on data roaming for the travel line.

Samsung / Android Setup Guide

  1. Open Settings.
  2. Go to Connections.
  3. Tap SIM Manager.
  4. Add eSIM.
  5. Scan QR code and enable mobile data.
These tools are estimates and beta previews. Compatibility, coverage, taxes, hotspot rules, and plan availability may change before launch.
